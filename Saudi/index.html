SAUDI FOUNDING DAY - 1727

Historical Roots

Founding Day honors the establishment of the First Saudi State in 1727 by Imam Muhammad bin Saud. Unlike National Day, which celebrates the 1932 unification, this day recognizes the 300-year-old political foundation laid in Diriyah.
